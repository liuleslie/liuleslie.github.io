This is a typographic animation of the (abridged) Navy Seal copypasta. Drawing on the visual language of fashwave, this animation leans into the hypermasculine ideals embodied in both the aesthetic and the copypasta itself to critique the former’s nostalgia for a time that never was and the latter’s tough guy front.
