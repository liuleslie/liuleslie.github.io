Inspired by Moniker’s Your Line or Mine and Clement Valla’s Mechanical Turk Alphabets, Betsy Ellison and I designed a set of three posters promoting a ouija board seance hosted by the St. Louis Paranormal Research Society.


Playing with the gesture of obscuring and revealing information, the posters mix clairvoyance with the occult, their symbols randomly chosen via a custom DrawBot program. The process through which the posters take form also involves an element of chance, as they invite passerby to roll a die to determine which symbols to draw over.

Fall 2021

24 × 36" each
