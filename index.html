Leslie Liu is a designer and aspiring morning person dedicated to serious and silly things. / Resume / Email / Colophon

I am interested in experiences that are magical, stuff that helps us reconsider intimacy and connection, as well as overlooked or ignored spaces online, in books, and beyond. Freshly graduated from Washington University in St. Louis, I’m now broadly based in the New York metropolitan area.

Thank you for looking at my work.
