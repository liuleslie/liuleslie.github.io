Weather

Summertime is for aestivation. I'm stewing about in a moat of confusion, awe, amusement, mild interest, numbness, joy, dismay, cheerfulness, bewilderment, and occasional apathy.

Focusing on outputs this season: how can I channel all this stuff I'm gesturing wildly about in a systematic and recognizable format? There is so much to learn: I would like to learn resilience and optimism.
