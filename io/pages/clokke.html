Ye Olde Status Clokke


Fall 2024

Particle Proton

A small status clock involving a microservo and a remix of my ever-stalled adaptation of Phoebe Anna Traquair’s writing hand.

The precedent for this was Isaac Blankensmith & Smooth Technology & co’s Paper Signals for Experiments with Google. I wanted to make something that likewise would be materially useful, and settled on this clock inspired by that of the metal status boards in some School of Art faculty/staff's offices in the basements on campus — as well as the Weasley family clock, from my Potterhead days long ago.

This project really humbled me — this was the first time I had to scramble and concretely think through how I’d be attaching things — though I achieved it (so jankily...). I also overthought the code; the process would’ve been much smoother if I’d understood more clearly how the servo updated its position between runs.

I’d like to make some laser cut, refined version of this sometime in the future.
