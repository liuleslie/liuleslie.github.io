Resisting Data-Driven Engineering of Our Ways of Being

How can we resist the engineering of our ways of being facilitated by rampant data collection for platform surveillance? To meaningfully practice curiosity; to fight pornification and anti-intellectualism.

  • How people/subjects and Big Data systems/platforms/infrastructures construct each other

  • How Big Data constructs normative subjects in American society

    • Cruel optimism: eroticized helplessness, privacy resignation; empowerment vs. agency, responsibility, ethics

    • Technology of behavior: convenience vs. friction, effort, labor, failure, curiosity

  • How Big Data constructs normative human-computation interaction paradigms

    • Carceral thinking: looking at the looking, law and order/decorum in digital engagement?

    • Narcissistic use: gazes and power dynamics
